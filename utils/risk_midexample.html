Now we proceed to the real trials. You will be presented with a spinner, which you can spin up to ten times, exactly as you did before.
Each time you spin it, you can either accept the result as a reward, or spin the spinner again in hopes of a higher reward.
Your bonus will be calculated by selecting the outcome of one random trial from the upcoming trials, and rewarding you according to your performance:
The closer your outcome is to the highest outcome in this trial the more money you will receive.

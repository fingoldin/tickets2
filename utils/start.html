1. Task: Training Phase


Stock prices for the company Flip or Skip are very volatile and the actual price does not give an indication for the next price.
In order to get familiar with the possible stock prices for Flip or Skip we will show you on the next screen a sequence of prices of potential stock prices.

At each stock price, press the spacebar or the 'Next' button to move to the next stock price.

After every 20 prices we will ask you about your estimate of the average stock price.

This will take about a minute.

Let's start!

In the next section you will encounter 80 sets of 10 tickets. The goal is to find the best ticket in every sequence, just as in the exmaple sequence.

You will get a monetary bonus that depends on your performance in these next trials.
The closer your chosen ticket is to the cheapest ticket in the sequence the more money you will receive.
If you choose the cheapest ticket in every sequence, you will earn the maximum bonus of $2.00.

Note: You can press the spacebar to go to the next ticket in each sequence during this phase.

Let's start!

In the next section you will encounter 200 consecutive sequences, each of 10 ticket prices, just as you saw before. The goal is to find the best ticket in every sequence.

You will get a monetary bonus that depends on your performance in these next 200 trials.
The closer your chosen ticket is to the cheapest ticket in the sequence the more money you will receive.
If you choose the cheapest ticket in every sequence, you get the maximum overall bonus of $4.00.

Note: You can press the spacebar to go to the next ticket or sequence of tickets during this phase.

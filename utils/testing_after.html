Let's start!

In the next section you will encounter 50 sets of 10 stocks. The goal is to find the highest price stock in every sequence, just as in the example sequence.

You will get a monetary bonus that depends on your performance in these next trials.
The closer your chosen stock is to the cheapest stock in the sequence the more money you will receive.
If you choose the most expensive stock in every sequence, you will earn the maximum bonus of $2.00.

Note: You can press the spacebar to go to the next stock in each sequence during this phase.

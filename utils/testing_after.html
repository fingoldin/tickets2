Let's start!

In the next section you will encounter 100 sets of 10 tickets, just as you saw before. The goal is to find the best ticket in every sequence.

You will get a monetary bonus that depends on your performance in these next trials.
The closer your chosen ticket is to the cheapest ticket in the sequence the more money you will receive.
If you choose the cheapest ticket in every sequence, you earn a bonus of $2.00.

Note: You can press the spacebar to go to the next ticket or sequence of tickets during this phase.

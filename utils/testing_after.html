Let's start!

In the next section you will encounter 50 consecutive sequences, each of 10 ticket prices, just as you saw before. The goal is to find the best ticket in every sequence.

You will get a monetary bonus that depends on your performance in these next 50 trials:
  • 3 points for picking the cheapest ticket
  • 2 points for picking the second cheapest ticket
  • 1 point for picking the third, forth or fifth cheapest ticket
  • 0 points otherwise

Your overall bonus will be proportional to your points, e.g. if you win all the points (300 points) you get $4.00, if you get half the points (150 points) you get $2.00.

Note: You can press the spacebar to go to the next ticket or sequence of tickets during this phase.

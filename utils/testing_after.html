Let's start!

In the next section you will encounter 120 products of 10 prices each. The goal is to find the best price for each product, just as in the trial sequence.

You will get a monetary bonus that depends on your performance in these next trials.
The closer your chosen price is to the cheapest price for this product the more money you will receive.
If you choose the cheapest product price in every trial, you will earn the maximum bonus of $4.00.

Note: You can press the spacebar to go to the next price in each sequence during this phase.

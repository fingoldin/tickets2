Now we proceed the real trials. Again, consider you're at the casino. You will do a series of trials of the following choice: either option 1, which will give you a fixed reward, or option 2, which will give you one of a set of rewards, with probabilities as shown in the pie chart. Your reward will be calculated by selecting the outcome of one random trial from the upcoming trials, and rewarding you the same number of cents as dollars in the outcome. For example, if the randomly chosen outcome was $180, you will earn $1.80.

Now we proceed to the real trials. Again, consider you're at the casino. You will do a series of games of the same choice that you have trained before:
either you choose the fixed reward, or you spin the wheel and receive the reward shown on the wheel.
Remember that the values shown on the pie chart are changing.

Your bonus will be calculated by selecting the outcome of one random game from the upcoming games, and rewarding you the same number of cents as dollars in the outcome. For example, if the randomly chosen outcome was $180, you will earn $1.80.

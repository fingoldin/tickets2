1.Task: Test Phase


In this section you will encounter sequences of price offers for your broken car, identical to the prices you experienced in the training phase.
For every price you can either accept it or decide to continue to the next one.
However, if you decline an offer from a particular scrapyard you cannot go back and sell it there anymore.
The goal is to find the highest offer in the sequence.

Pressing the spacebar will go to the next price, or will choose the current one if it is the last in the sequence.

NOTE: The first following sequence is an example sequence, and while you should try to find the best price, your performance in this sequence does NOT matter regarding your final payment.

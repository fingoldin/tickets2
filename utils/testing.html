You will see a picture of the product in the middle of the screen. This is the product you want to buy, however, the prices of the product can change over time. The price fluctuations in this experiment correspond to the real-life price volatility of each product. We further provide an average price of the product in the left top corner.

Pressing the spacebar will go to the next price, or will choose the current one if it is the last in the sequence.
